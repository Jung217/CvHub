Abstract 摘要

We report surprising observations that challenge the conventional wisdom of ImageNet pre-training. Our experiments show that ImageNet pre-training speeds up convergence early in training, but does not necessarily provide regularization or improve final target task accuracy for object detection and instance segmentation. Models trained from random initialization (from scratch) on the target dataset can be no worse than their pre-trained counterparts, as long as they are trained for long enough.
我們報告了挑戰 ImageNet 預訓練傳統認知的驚人發現。實驗表明，ImageNet 預訓練加速了訓練初期的收斂，但不一定為物體偵測和實例分割提供正則化或改善最終目標任務精度。只要訓練足夠長時間，以隨機初始化（從頭訓練）在目標資料集上訓練的模型可以不遜於預訓練的對應模型。

These observations hold across multiple tasks (object detection, instance segmentation, and keypoint detection), multiple datasets (COCO and PASCAL VOC), and multiple network architectures. Our results are not meant to discredit ImageNet pre-training, which is still useful for speeding up convergence and for scenarios with limited target data. Rather, we aim to recalibrate the community's understanding of what pre-training provides.

1. Introduction 緒論

ImageNet pre-training followed by fine-tuning has become the de facto paradigm in computer vision. Since the pioneering work of Krizhevsky et al. (AlexNet), it has been commonly believed that pre-training on ImageNet provides universal visual features that significantly benefit downstream tasks. This belief has been reinforced by consistent empirical evidence across many tasks and datasets.
ImageNet 預訓練再微調已成為電腦視覺的既定範式。自 Krizhevsky 等人（AlexNet）的開創性工作以來，人們普遍認為在 ImageNet 上的預訓練提供了對下游任務有顯著幫助的通用視覺特徵。這一信念已被多個任務和資料集上一致的經驗證據所強化。

邏輯角色 建立靶子：越是根深蒂固的信念，挑戰它的價值越大
論證技巧 以 AlexNet 這個里程碑追溯信念的起源，讓讀者意識到這個假設已有近十年歷史，暗示有重新檢驗的必要
However, we question whether ImageNet pre-training is truly necessary for achieving strong performance on target tasks, particularly when the target dataset is sufficiently large (e.g., COCO). Recent advances in normalization techniques (such as Group Normalization and Synchronized Batch Normalization) have made training from scratch more feasible by addressing the small batch size problem that previously made random initialization impractical for detection and segmentation tasks.
然而，我們質疑 ImageNet 預訓練對於在目標任務上達到強勁效能是否真正必要，特別是當目標資料集足夠大（如 COCO）時。近期在正規化技術（如群組正規化和同步批次正規化）方面的進展，透過解決之前使隨機初始化在偵測和分割任務中不實際的小批次問題，使從頭訓練變得更加可行。

3. Experimental Methodology 實驗方法

We conduct experiments using Mask R-CNN and Faster R-CNN with Feature Pyramid Networks (FPN) on the COCO dataset. For each configuration, we compare two training strategies: (1) fine-tuning from ImageNet pre-trained weights, the standard practice, and (2) training from random initialization (scratch) using appropriate normalization. For the scratch models, we use Group Normalization (GN) or Synchronized Batch Normalization (SyncBN) instead of frozen BN layers, which is critical for stable training.
我們使用 Mask R-CNN 和 Faster R-CNN 搭配特徵金字塔網路（FPN）在 COCO 資料集上進行實驗。對於每個配置，我們比較兩種訓練策略：（1）從 ImageNet 預訓練權重微調，即標準做法；（2）使用適當正規化的隨機初始化訓練（從頭訓練）。對於從頭訓練的模型，我們使用群組正規化（GN）或同步批次正規化（SyncBN）取代凍結的 BN 層，這對穩定訓練至關重要。

A key aspect of our methodology is the training schedule. We observe that models trained from scratch require significantly longer training schedules to converge. While the standard fine-tuning schedule uses 90k iterations (1x schedule), we train from-scratch models for up to 6x longer (540k iterations). We also explore various hyperparameter settings including learning rate warmup, gradient clipping, and data augmentation strategies to ensure fair comparison.
方法論的一個關鍵面向是訓練排程。我們觀察到從頭訓練的模型需要顯著更長的訓練排程才能收斂。標準微調排程使用 9 萬次迭代（1x 排程），而我們將從頭訓練的模型訓練長達 6 倍（54 萬次迭代）。我們也探索了包括學習率預熱、梯度裁剪和資料擴增策略在內的各種超參數設定，以確保比較的公平性。

4. Results 實驗結果

Our main finding is striking: with sufficient training time, models trained from scratch can match the accuracy of ImageNet pre-trained models on COCO. Specifically, Mask R-CNN with ResNet-50 FPN trained from scratch achieves 39.0 AP for object detection, compared to 38.9 AP for the pre-trained counterpart. For instance segmentation, the scratch model achieves 35.0 AP vs. 35.4 AP. The differences are within statistical noise.
我們的主要發現非常驚人：在足夠的訓練時間下，從頭訓練的模型能在 COCO 上匹配 ImageNet 預訓練模型的精度。具體而言，從頭訓練的 Mask R-CNN 搭配 ResNet-50 FPN 在物體偵測上達到 39.0 AP，而預訓練對應模型為 38.9 AP。在實例分割上，從頭訓練模型達到 35.0 AP 對比 35.4 AP。差異在統計雜訊範圍內。

We further observe that ImageNet pre-training does not help if the target task has enough data. On COCO with its 118k training images, from-scratch training matches pre-trained models. However, when we reduce the COCO training set to 10k or 35k images, pre-training provides a clear advantage. This suggests that the main benefit of pre-training is as a form of data augmentation when target data is scarce, rather than providing fundamentally better feature representations.
我們進一步觀察到，當目標任務有足夠資料時，ImageNet 預訓練並無幫助。在擁有 118k 訓練影像的 COCO 上，從頭訓練與預訓練模型匹敵。然而，當我們將 COCO 訓練集縮減至 10k 或 35k 張影像時，預訓練提供了明顯優勢。這表明預訓練的主要效益是在目標資料稀缺時作為一種資料擴增，而非提供本質上更好的特徵表徵。

We also find that these observations hold across different architectures. With ResNet-101 backbone, the scratch model achieves 41.0 AP compared to 40.6 AP for the pre-trained model in object detection on COCO. We acknowledge that pre-training still provides substantial practical benefits: it reduces training time by roughly 3x-5x and requires less careful hyperparameter tuning. These practical advantages should not be dismissed.
我們也發現這些觀察在不同架構下均成立。使用 ResNet-101 骨幹，從頭訓練模型在 COCO 物體偵測上達到 41.0 AP，而預訓練模型為 40.6 AP。我們承認預訓練仍提供顯著的實務效益：它將訓練時間縮減約 3-5 倍，且需要較少的超參數調整。這些實務優勢不應被忽視。

5. Conclusion 結論

Our experiments demonstrate that ImageNet pre-training is not necessary for achieving strong performance on COCO object detection and segmentation when sufficient target data and training time are available. The primary benefit of pre-training is faster convergence rather than improved final accuracy. These findings suggest that the dominant role of ImageNet in the transfer learning paradigm should be reconsidered, and that investing in larger target datasets and longer training schedules may be equally valuable.
實驗證明，當有足夠的目標資料和訓練時間時，ImageNet 預訓練對於在 COCO 物體偵測和分割上達到強勁效能並非必要。預訓練的主要效益是加速收斂而非改善最終精度。這些發現表明，ImageNet 在遷移學習範式中的主導地位應被重新審視，投資於更大的目標資料集和更長的訓練排程可能同等有價值。

We hope our work encourages the community to explore alternative approaches to the standard pre-training paradigm. As normalization techniques continue to improve and target datasets grow larger, the gap between pre-trained and from-scratch models may continue to narrow. We believe that understanding the true mechanisms of transfer learning is crucial for advancing the field beyond empirical recipe-following.
我們希望本研究鼓勵社群探索標準預訓練範式的替代方案。隨著正規化技術持續改進和目標資料集日益增大，預訓練模型與從頭訓練模型之間的差距可能繼續縮小。我們認為，理解遷移學習的真正機制對於推動領域超越經驗性的方法套用至關重要。

核心主張

ImageNet 預訓練對於 COCO 等大規模目標資料集上的物體偵測和分割並非必要；在足夠的訓練時間和適當的正規化技術下，從頭訓練可達到同等精度，預訓練的主要價值僅在於加速收斂。

論證最強處

跨架構（ResNet-50/101）、跨任務（偵測/分割/關鍵點）、跨資料集（COCO/VOC）的一致性結果，搭配不同資料量的控制實驗，系統性極高。特別是資料量消融實驗精確定位了預訓練有效的邊界條件。

論證最弱處

從頭訓練需要 3-5 倍的訓練時間和更仔細的超參數調整，這在實際研究中是顯著的成本。論文未充分量化這些額外成本（GPU 時數、碳排放），使「是否值得從頭訓練」的實際決策缺乏完整的成本效益分析。
